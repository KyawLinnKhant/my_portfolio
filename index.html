About Me

I'm Kyaw Linn Khant — a Physical AI & Robotics Engineer with a BEng (Hons) in Robotics and Artificial Intelligence from the University of Hertfordshire. I build systems that sit at the intersection of embodied intelligence and real-world deployment — from Sim2Real RL policies running on microcontrollers to autonomous agents managing Kubernetes clusters.

My work spans the full stack: training RL and ML models, deploying them at the edge with TFLite and TensorRT, integrating LiDAR SLAM and sensor fusion pipelines in ROS2, and building LLM-powered agentic systems with LangChain, LangGraph, and RAG. I care about systems that actually work outside the lab.

I've delivered projects for clients across Singapore as a freelance R&D consultant, covering physical AI, robotics prototyping, and embedded systems — working independently from brief to deployment.
