About Me

Hi, I'm Kyaw Linn Khant, a robotics and AI enthusiast. I have successfully accomplished my Bachelor of Engineering (Honours) in Robotics and Artificial Intelligence at the University of Hertfordshire [Aug 2025].

My fascination with robotics began when I was a kid playing with an Optimus Prime transformable figure. That early spark grew into a passion for designing and programming intelligent robots that can interact with the world in meaningful ways.

Over the years, I've expanded my interests into computer vision, reinforcement learning, and machine learning applications across robotics and AI. I enjoy exploring new ideas, building projects, and continuously improving my skills.
